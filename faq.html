How can you print a document fromt your laptop at HZ?

Log in at my hz. Edit the personal menu. Add the self service portal. Go to the self service portal. Click reserve .

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Make sure you have a sufficient amount of money on your HZ card. Register using your HZ pass on the RFID reader next to the printer. In the printer menu, select option Scan. Place the original sheet on the feeder or glass plate. Press send to me. Press Yes. Press the Start button. Press the green Start button on the printer to scan page by page. Once this is ready, press Start Sending.

What are the instructions if you want to park you car at the HZ parking lot?
